Visit Ballarshah

Enjoy the culture and vibrant beauty of our town near distict Chandrapur also known as black gold city.

Top three places to vist at Ballarshah

Tadoba National Park

Tiger is the main attraction of this park and the tourists can see them occasionally in the deep forest or near the lake. It is one of the most popular tiger reserves in India, known for best tiger sightings.

Mahakali Mandir

The ancient temple was built by Dhundya Ram Sah, Tribal King of Gond Dynasty around 16th century. The sculptures on the walls of Mahakali temple were outstanding blend of Tibetan and Hindu traditions.

Big Wood of Ballarshah

It is 15.621 meter long massive teak wood.
